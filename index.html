Breeding Records

üí° How Breeding Records Work

Record when you put a buck with your does, and the app will automatically fill in the sire when you add kidding records later!

  • Create a breeding record with the date, sire (buck), and which does were exposed
  • When you add kidding records 145-155 days later, the sire will auto-populate
  • The app looks for breeding records within 180 days before the kidding date
  • You can have multiple breeding records over time - the most recent one before kidding is used
